Guild History

1985

Guild Founded

Bettery Miller and 11 other local quilters met for the first time in Betty's living room. The mission was simple: gather and stitch.

1990

First Annual Show

With 50 quilts exhibited at the community hall, our ì²« (first) annual show was a massive success, drawing 200 visitors.

2000

Library Established

We began our technical library with 500 donated books and patterns, now a cornerstone of our member benefits.

2025

40th Anniversary

Today we stand 300+ members strong, celebrating four decades of quilting excellence and community service.
