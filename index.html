Frequently Asked Questions

What skill level are these patterns suitable for?

Our patterns range from beginner to advanced. Each pattern includes a difficulty rating and detailed instructions suitable for the specified skill level.

Can I sell quilts made from these patterns?

Most free patterns allow for personal use and small-scale selling. Always check the specific pattern's licensing terms before selling items made from the pattern.

What fabric should I use for my first quilt?

We recommend 100% cotton quilting fabric for beginners. It's easy to work with, presses well, and creates durable quilts.

How do I choose the right batting?

Consider your quilt's intended use. Cotton batting is traditional and breathable, while polyester provides more warmth and is lighter.

What size should my quilt backing be?

Your backing should be 4-6 inches larger than your quilt top on all sides to allow for quilting and trimming.

How do I prevent fabric fraying?

Use sharp tools, handle fabric gently, and consider using fray check on edges that will receive heavy use.
