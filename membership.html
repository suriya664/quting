Join Our Quilting Family

Member Benefits

Monthly Meetings

First Tuesday of every month. Social hour, program, and show-and-tell. Refreshments provided.

Workshops & Classes

Access to monthly hands-on workshops with materials included at discounted member rates.

Library Access

Borrow from our collection of 1,500+ books, patterns, and DVDs for technique mastery.

Annual Quilt Show

Members enter quilts for free. Compete for cash prizes, ribbons, and public recognition.

Shop Discounts

20% off at local fabric stores and 10% off professional long-arm quilting services.

Charity Projects

Participate in sewing bees making quilts for hospitals and shelters. We provide the fabric!
